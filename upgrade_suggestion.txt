2. Add Open Graph metadata

In every page's <head>:

<meta property="og:title" content="Complete Care Homoeo">
<meta property="og:description" content="Personalized Homoeopathic Care in Bangalore">
<meta property="og:image" content="https://completecarehomoeo.in/assets/images/dr-shrinidhi.webp">
<meta property="og:url" content="https://completecarehomoeo.in">
<meta property="og:type" content="website">

This makes WhatsApp, Facebook, LinkedIn, etc. show a nice preview when someone shares your site.
